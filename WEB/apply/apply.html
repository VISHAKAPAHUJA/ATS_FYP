Login Instructions

If you are already registered on STS Candidate Portal, then enter your CNIC Number and 6-digit password to login to your candidate portal.
If you are not registered on STS Candidate Portal, click on 'Create an Account' link below to create a new account.
If you are registered on STS Candidate Portal, but you have forgotten your password, then click on 'Forget Password' link to recover your password.
